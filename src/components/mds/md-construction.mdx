# Dataset construction

![](./pic/pipeline.jpg)

**Overview of Garments2Look construction process.** Our dataset follows four steps: (1) Data Collection: obtaining realworld clothing items and their outfit suggestions from different sources; (2) Data Synthesis: enriching the dataset content and diversity by generating new outfit lists and look
images; (3) Data Filtering: ensuring visual consistency and data quality, including annotations of garment images, outfit lists and look images; and (4) Data Evaluation: verifying the data quality, designing new metrics for outfit-level VTON task, and testing SOTA models.
