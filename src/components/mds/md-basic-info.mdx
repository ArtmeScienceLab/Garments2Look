# Dataset Basic Info

![](./pic/examples.jpg)

**Data structure.** This dataset provides high-quality, high-precision, and highly diverse samples of complex and complete clothing combinations.

![](./pic/basic-data-info.jpg)

**Data distribution statistics for Garments2Look.** The real and synthetic data in the final dataset are maintained at ∼1:1 ratio. We collect data covering diverse genders, different numbers of garment images per outfit, different layering order lengths, and encompassing a broad range of garments categories and outfit combination patterns. We also pay attention to textual annotation to facilitate future multimodal research.
